# 📊 Marketing Data Analyst Internship

## 📌 Overview  
Worked as a Marketing Data Analyst Intern at Anteriad, focusing on data analysis, reporting, and dashboard development to support campaign performance and business decision-making.

---

## 🚀 Key Highlights  

- Analyzed campaign performance using **Google Ads, Bing Ads, and Google Analytics** to identify trends and optimize campaigns  
- Built and maintained **pacing and performance reports** to track budget allocation and campaign efficiency  
- Created reports across different timeframes (**weekly, MTD, YTD**) to compare performance and support data-driven decisions  
- Conducted **URL verification and web scraping validation**, improving data quality and identifying issues in AI-generated data 
- Designed and improved **Power BI dashboards** to enhance data visualization and client understanding  
- Used **SQL for SKU matching and data validation**, ensuring accurate product tracking and reporting  
- Managed and organized data from multiple sources to support campaign services and customer data platforms  

---

## 🛠️ Tools  
`SQL` • `Power BI` • `Excel` • `Google Ads` • `Google Analytics`  

---

## 📊 Impact  
- Improved data accuracy and reporting reliability through validation and analysis  
- Supported campaign optimization by identifying high and low-performing segments  
- Enhanced data storytelling through clear dashboards and visualizations  
- Strengthened ability to work with real-world, messy datasets
